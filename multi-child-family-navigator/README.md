# Multi-Child Family Navigator

**An Interactive Assessment Tool by The Quiet Edge**

## About

This interactive tool helps parents of multiple young athletes navigate the complex dynamics of supporting several children in sports. Based on sibling dynamics research and developmental psychology, it provides personalized strategies for common multi-child challenges.

## Features

- **Family Composition Assessment** - Understand your unique family dynamics
- **Sibling Dynamics Evaluation** - Identify comparison patterns and rivalry triggers
- **Scenario-Specific Guidance** - Address common multi-child challenges:
  - Same sport, different skill levels
  - One child wanting to quit
  - Younger sibling surpassing older
  - Resource imbalance
  - Schedule conflicts
  - Toxic comparison
  - Identity issues
- **Personalized Action Plans** - Get research-based strategies for your situation
- **Fair Attention Framework** - Balance support across all children

## How to Use

Visit the live site and work through each step of the assessment. The tool will generate a personalized report based on your family's specific circumstances.

## Research Foundation

This tool incorporates findings from peer-reviewed research including:
- Côté (1999) - Family influence on talent development
- Davis & Meyer (2008) - Same-sex sibling competition in elite sport
- Hopwood et al. (2015) - Sibling dynamics and sport expertise
- Sulloway (2007) - Birth order and sibling competition
