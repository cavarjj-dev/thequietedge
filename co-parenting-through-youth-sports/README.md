# Co-Parenting Through Youth Sports

**An Interactive Assessment Tool by The Quiet Edge**

## About

This interactive tool helps separated or divorced parents navigate the unique challenges of co-parenting through their child's youth sports experience. Based on research-backed approaches, it provides personalized assessments and action plans.

## Features

- **Co-Parenting Model Assessment** - Identify your current co-parenting style and its impact
- **Warning Signs Checklist** - Recognize when your child may be struggling
- **Communication Protocols** - Establish effective information sharing systems
- **Decision Frameworks** - Navigate day-to-day, minor, and major decisions
- **Personalized Action Plans** - Get specific 4-week implementation guidance
- **Situation-Specific Guidance** - Address your unique challenges

## How to Use

Visit the live site and work through each step of the assessment. The tool will generate a personalized report with research-based recommendations.

## Research Foundation

This tool incorporates findings from peer-reviewed research on co-parenting, child development, and youth sports psychology.
